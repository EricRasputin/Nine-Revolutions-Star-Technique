# Chapter 10: Reckless Human Madman

"Human brat, your arrogance has gone too far. This King is the Purple Lightning Mad Lion, and to dare insult this King's name is unforgivable!"

The Purple Lightning Mad Lion was fully enraged now. The proud bloodline inside it could not stand to see its name scorned, and a sky-flooding demonic aura erupted from its body.

It threw its enormous head back and roared at the heavens.

Birds and beasts scattered in every direction.

Watching the lion's presence surge again, Su Yang felt no fear at all.

He struck first. His body blurred forward, mountain-shaking force erupting in an instant as he smashed toward the Purple Lightning Mad Lion. "Hyah!" His blood boiled, and a stunning aura burst from every pore in his body.

"This King wants you dead!" Seeing Su Yang attack first, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion opened its bloody maw and slammed its front claws forward.

The two forces collided in an instant, sending out wave after wave of pressure.

Boom!

After that muffled crash, Su Yang kept swinging his fists. Even with his arms trembling, his eyes were already filled with madness.

The Purple Lightning Mad Lion wasn't afraid in the slightest. As a rank-six demonic beast, its physical strength far surpassed any human cultivator at the same level.

The two went back and forth, fist for claw, claw for fist.

The savage exchange left Su Yang covered in claw marks. The Purple Lightning Mad Lion, for its part, had been hammered backward several times by Su Yang's fists with their ten-thousand-jin weight, its head split open and demonic blood streaming down.

Yet neither human nor beast felt pain anymore. Both had slipped into a frenzy.

Su Yang fought to grow stronger. The Purple Lightning Mad Lion had absolute faith in its own body. Neither feared physical pain. Each wanted to pound the other into dust.

"Yayayah!" Su Yang kept swinging his fists, his combat aura soaring to its peak.

His boiling bloodline had long since pushed pain out of his head. Only ceaseless battle could fuel ceaseless growth.

Su Yang's fist slammed into the Purple Lightning Mad Lion's belly, and even that mighty demonic body caved in under the blow. The lion roared and threw itself forward, heedless of its wounds, vicious claws raking savagely at Su Yang.

Su Yang flickered aside in time, dodging the fatal blow. Even so, three deep claw marks now ran across his chest, and golden blood began to pour from them...

In the lion's mind, that kind of wound was enough to cripple any weak human. The human cultivators it had fought before couldn't survive a single swipe before dropping dead on the spot. Even a third-rank Divine Martial Realm expert had nearly been killed by it once. If that human hadn't been too cunning and tricked it, there was no way it would have escaped.

Yet the human youth in front of it, even after taking that terrifying claw, showed no sign of backing down.

As if pain meant nothing to him, his ten-thousand-jin fists kept hammering down on it like rain.

Even its head was starting to buzz.

*Damn it, this kid isn't human at all. He doesn't fear pain in the slightest?* The Purple Lightning Mad Lion had never met an opponent this savage. Even fighting other rank-six beasts for territory, things had never gotten this brutal.

The lion that had come at him so aggressively was now starting to lose its nerve.

It honestly couldn't make sense of it. Did this human youth feel no pain at all? Had his very flesh transcended mortal limits?

How could the Purple Lightning Mad Lion know? It wasn't that Su Yang felt no pain. The conviction inside him, the hatred inside him, was the only thing holding him up. *I have to grow stronger. I have to keep growing stronger. Every last soul in the Wang Manor has to die!*

As time wore on, not a single part of Su Yang's body was unhurt. His face, his chest, his back, even his fists had twisted into horrifying shapes, as if every bone had been knocked out of place...

Yet he never stopped his attack. He kept fighting like a wild beast, blood pouring without end, the enemy stronger than him, and with every strike he only grew braver still.

Even the Great Sage in the stone was shaken by what Su Yang had become. "Good kid. Truly fierce. The Battle Sage Art will surely unleash its full power through you, boy. Whether this Great Sage gets out from under this starry sky one day... might just hinge on you..."

"Aiyah!" Su Yang kept attacking. His death-defying assault was finally starting to seize the upper hand.

After all, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion was a rank-six demonic beast. Even after taking countless heavy fists from Su Yang, its speed had hardly dropped. It had simply lost its nerve. It had no interest in being chased and hammered by a human madman, much less in trading wounds with him to the bitter end.

It wanted to slip away, to retreat deep into the mountains and recover...

The humans from the outside really were terrifying. No wonder mama had told it never to provoke them lightly, especially the kind who didn't fear death.

The moment a creature starts thinking about backing down, its presence collapses in an instant. The Purple Lightning Mad Lion was no exception.

Su Yang's frenzied assault left the Purple Lightning Mad Lion with no will to keep fighting. It only wanted to retreat to its own territory.

Lucky for it, all the rank-six and above beasts in the area had already been driven off. If word of today's events got out, it might never hold its place in the Demonic Beast Mountain Range again.

The Purple Lightning Mad Lion was furious at the thought, dying to swat Su Yang dead with one strike. But it couldn't.

If the fight kept going, even if both sides came away wounded, even if it killed the human youth in front of it, what kind of state would it be in by the end? An old rival might catch it weakened, and the outcome would only be uglier.

The thought alone made the Purple Lightning Mad Lion shudder from head to tail.

It deliberately took a fist from Su Yang, and as its body flew back, another Purple Lightning Laser Cannon ripped from its mouth. It meant to use the instant Su Yang spent blocking to escape into the depths of the range.

Su Yang was just hitting his stride. Seeing the lion switch tactics, he froze where he stood, and that terrible force was bearing down on him in the next breath.

The Great Sage's voice rang in his mind. "Kid, snap out of it. This beast is already terrified of your reckless aura. While he's like this, latch on, get him into the stone."

The words sent fresh fire into Su Yang's veins.

He shot his right fist forward, throwing every ounce of strength in his body straight at the Purple Lightning Laser Cannon.

Seeing this, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion broke into curses. "Damn, this guy's really a maniac. Yours truly's done playing along."

"Knock yourself out, you bastard.[^1]"

With that, the wings on its back burst into motion, and the lion shot away as a streak of purple lightning.

Su Yang's punch tore the lion's attack clean in two. He passed straight through the middle of it, untouched.

Seeing the Purple Lightning Mad Lion try to bolt, was Su Yang about to let that slide?

He let out a furious roar at once. "Little lion! Where do you think you're going!"

And then he too became a streak of golden lightning, blazing after the lion.

Human and beast tore through the Demonic Beast Mountain Range, one chasing, the other fleeing. The Purple Lightning Mad Lion felt like it was about to vomit blood. It had been sure it could shake off the death-defying youth without breaking a sweat. Who could have guessed that this kid, whose realm looked like nothing special, would be this fast on his feet?

Not only was Su Yang keeping pace. He was actually closing in.

The Purple Lightning Mad Lion had never questioned its life choices the way it did today...

---

[^1]: 你丫的 (nǐ yā de) — Beijing-dialect vulgarity, originally a contraction of 你丫头养的 (literally "you, raised by a slave girl"). The 丫 character is the live marker; modern usage drops the literal meaning and just lands as a punchy "you bastard" or "you piece of shit." Coming from the lion, the word is loaded — the same beast was calling itself 本王 ("this King") two paragraphs earlier. Its pomposity is collapsing into gutter slang as it bolts.

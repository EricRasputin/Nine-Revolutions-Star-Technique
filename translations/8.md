# Chapter 8: Purple Lightning Mad Lion

A savage combat aura blanketed the sky.

Countless blurs of staff motion surged forward, sweeping in unison to meet the four Wang Manor men's assault.

"This kid—how can his combat aura be this overwhelming?"

"Damn it, he's only at the True Martial Realm. How can he be this terrifying? What kind of cultivation method is this? I've never seen anything like it..."

A flood of thoughts rushed through the bearded brute's mind. He could never have imagined that the youth before him, hemmed in by his own assault and that of three other True Martial Realm experts, could still erupt with such a terrifying combat aura, his staff blurring with strikes that came too fast to track.

Boom! Boom! Boom!

Within that raging storm of strikes, Su Yang's dominance was absolute: he felled each of the three Wang Manor True Martial Realm experts with a single, decisive blow.

Three blood-curdling screams tore through the sky at once.

Then silence. Three corpses dropped out of thin air and slammed down beside the stream...

A closer look revealed how horribly the three had died: not a single skull intact, every one smashed to a pulp, no chance of survival.

The bearded brute himself was now squaring off against the overpowering storm of Su Yang's staff.

His mountainous frame slammed into that storm.

*Crack!* The brittle reports of snapping bone rang out one after another, and a single staff sweep sent the bearded brute's body flying.

Blood erupted from his mouth, mouthful after mouthful.

That mountainous frame tumbled through the air several times before plunging toward the ground...

With a thunderous crash, the bearded brute landed half-kneeling, blood pouring from his mouth, his aura fading fast.

Su Yang himself dropped from the air just then, wooden staff in hand, walking slowly toward the brute.

The brute stared at Su Yang, his eyes already brimming with terror, as if the youth in front of him were no human, but a demon up from hell itself.

"You... y-you... what on earth—"

"Bwah—!"

Before he could finish, the brute spat out another mouthful of blood.

He collapsed flat on the ground...

Su Yang looked down at the brute, his face expressionless. "This Young Master will spare your life. Whether you make it out of the Demonic Beast Mountain Range or not is up to your luck. If you do make it back to Wang Manor, kindly let your Eldest Miss know that I, Su Yang... am still alive."

At those words, the brute's pupils blew wide once more. He raised a trembling right hand and pointed at Su Yang. "You... y-you... you're—"

Su Yang paid him no further mind and turned away.

A few flickers and he was gone, swallowed by the dense forest.

"Not bad. Wrapping up the fight that fast didn't disappoint this Great Sage. Still, it's not quite perfect. At your current realm, you could've crushed that Tri-Profound Realm cultivator with a single staff blow. Why let him live?" The Great Sage's voice rose in Su Yang's mind.

Su Yang was silent for a moment. Then he answered slowly, "The Wang Manor must be drunk on their joy right now, especially the Wang Family Patriarch and that vicious woman. As long as I, Su Yang, am still alive, I will not let them know peace."

"Senior Great Sage, please ramp the training up. I can't wait any longer to take my vengeance."

Su Yang's fists clenched tight as he spoke.

Two figures rose in his mind just then, his father and mother.

The Su Family had to be in pieces by now, his parents almost certainly suffering for it. A moment ago Su Yang had meant to question those Wang Manor men about exactly that. They had been too eager to die, though, so he had let it go.

He'd be leaving the Demonic Beast Mountain Range before long anyway, and once he made the trip to the Su Family in person, the truth would surface soon enough.

"Kid, be careful. That demonic beast has appeared!" At that very moment, the Great Sage's voice grew serious.

At those words Su Yang stopped where he stood, tension flooding in despite himself.

The demonic beast the Great Sage spoke of was the very one that had appeared three months ago and started mauling people across the range.

Only Su Yang hadn't run into it all this while, or to put it more accurately, he had always slipped away in the nick of time, with the beast hurrying in moments later.

Today, with the stench of blood thick on him, had the beast finally tracked him down?

Su Yang's heart sank; for the Great Sage to be this alert, the beast was no ordinary one, at the very least rank-five and possibly rank-six or higher.

With Su Yang's current strength, victory was far from guaranteed, and even if he did win, both sides would walk away wounded.

That would push his grand revenge plan back by who knows how long...

And once Wang Yiyi left Yebei City, who could say how long he would have to wait for another shot at vengeance?

Su Yang did not want to burn too much of his strength now on dealing with that terrifying beast.

But the choice was no longer his, since in the past, after killing a demonic beast, Su Yang had always made a point of going to the nearby stream to wash off the stench of blood. This time, after dispatching the Wang Manor experts, his anger had pushed the thought clean out of his head, and he had simply turned and walked away.

*It was careless of me. Not that anyone could really blame me; any guy told by a woman he can't perform would have his head on fire too, wouldn't he? And then to be cast out in front of everyone... it's viciousness taken to the extreme, shameless to the bone!!*

Still, the fighting spirit from his clash with the Wang Manor experts hadn't faded in him; if anything, his grip on the wooden staff tightened by the second.

The Supreme Bloodline kept boiling.

"Roaaaaar—"

A demonic beast's roar erupted from the woods on Su Yang's right, and before he could turn his head a black silhouette came hurtling at him.

Bam!

Su Yang's reaction was instant; he flickered back at once, and in that same heartbeat the earth shook and the mountains trembled, dust rising everywhere.

A powerful demonic aura, mingled with the dust, flooded the sky.

One look and Su Yang was startled by the sight before him: an enormous bear-lion, its whole body cast in purple. Its long fur crackled with thunderous power, and a pair of long wings wreathed in blue flame stretched from its back, the whole creature looking nothing short of magnificent.

At this moment, two jade-blue eyes were locked dead on Su Yang, the beast letting out a low growl every so often.

The pressure of a king rolled off it, fearsome to face.

"Damn... what kind of demonic beast is this? It looks pretty wild." Su Yang had never seen anything like it. From its sheer presence alone, he could feel a domineering aura pouring off the beast.

"Tsk-tsk, kid, you're in luck after all. To run into a rank-six demonic beast in a backwater like this, called... what was it now... let this Great Sage think for a bit..."

"...Senior, please don't mess with me right now. Just tell me, do I fight or run...?" Su Yang felt close to tears with no tears to shed. Who would have thought the beast in front of him would actually turn out to be a rank-six?

A rank-six demonic beast was on par with a Divine Martial Realm expert at Perfection.

Right now he couldn't reliably put down even a Divine Martial Realm cultivator, let alone the savage-looking rank-six beast in front of him.

"Easy now, this Great Sage just remembered. This must be a mutated Purple Lightning Mad Lion. Yes, that's it, Purple Lightning Mad Lion; by all rights it shouldn't be showing up in a mountain range like this, which is genuinely strange." The Great Sage sounded a touch puzzled.

Just then, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion let out another roar. It crept forward on its four powerful limbs, body low to the ground, on the verge of lunging.

Su Yang dared not let his guard down; he was already gripping his staff tight, golden light shimmering again across his frame. Every part of him was braced for a fierce battle...

"Kid, didn't you say you wanted revenge? If you can subdue this Purple Lightning Mad Lion, that's not a bad option."

"Shall we give it a shot?"

# Chapter 7: Clash with the Wang Manor Experts!

"Enough chatter. Let's get on with the real work. The Patriarch sent us here to track down that demonic beast that's been wounding people, put it down, and win Yebei City over."

"Even the City Lord's Manor couldn't handle this one. If we're the ones to settle it, our Wang Manor's reputation is going to soar all over again," said one of the Wang Manor men, a hulking fellow with a thick stubbled beard.

Su Yang was in no rush to act. From a hidden perch in the trees, he sized up the strength of the Wang Manor crew.

*One Tri-Profound Realm cultivator and four in the ninth-grade True Martial Realm. The Wang Family's really gone all out.* Su Yang could feel that out of the five, only the bearded brute carried a Tri-Profound Realm cultivation base. The other four posed no real threat.

If he moved himself, taking down all of them was hardly out of the question.

And while he was at it, he'd remind the Wang Family that Su Yang was still very much around.

The thought brought his grip tight around the wooden staff, and the Supreme Bloodline within him began to slowly stir.

"Kid, this is the first time in three months you'll be fighting actual cultivators, isn't it? It's a good chance to sharpen yourself. Demonic beasts can boost your combat strength, sure, but at the end of the day they're just animals. Cultivators are a different story. Don't forget, human cunning is in the blood."

"Let this Sage see whether these three months of bitter training have earned my approval."

The Great Sage's voice rose again in his mind. Su Yang's lips curled as a wave of confidence rose within him. "Just watch, Senior. I, Su Yang, will not disappoint you."

With that, Su Yang began to stalk forward like a leopard closing on its prey.

The five Wang Family experts had just been about to leave the stream and resume their hunt for the man-killing demonic beast. The instant they turned, they spotted a youth with a wooden staff in hand, eyes locked on them.

"Gentlemen, you came all this way. No need to leave so soon, is there?" Su Yang said with a smile as he watched the five.

The five Wang Family experts froze. Who would have imagined that a green-eared kid would crop up in the middle of the Demonic Beast Mountain Range?

"Where did you crawl out of, brat? Don't you know who we are?" snapped one of them, a short, wiry man, jabbing a finger at Su Yang.

The bearded brute, however, was far steadier. As the Tri-Profound Realm expert of the group, his perception was without question the sharpest among them. And yet, he had not sensed even a flicker of warning before this kid appeared. That alone was proof enough that this was no ordinary boy.

What more, the bearded brute couldn't shake the feeling that the youth in front of him looked oddly familiar.

But for the life of him, he couldn't place where he'd seen him before.

"Know? Aren't you just the Wang Family's lap dogs?" Su Yang said in a tone of ice, killing intent plain on his face.

"Brat, you're courting death!" The short man flew into a rage at his words. His slight, scrawny body suddenly lunged at Su Yang with the speed of a wild beast.

Su Yang did not so much as twitch.

*A clash of bodies? Hmph!*

There was a thunderous crash, and the short expert was sent hurtling backward in an instant. The right fist he had thrown was now utterly shattered, dripping blood.

"AHHHH!!!"

"My hand!!!" The short man hit the ground howling, rolling in agony.

The other four were dumbstruck. As one, their eyes turned to Su Yang, who stood there smirking, not a hair out of place. They knew full well that the short man was a perfected True Martial Realm expert, fearsome enough by the standards of all of Yebei City.

And yet this youth, without so much as a true strike, had already crippled him in an instant?

What in the world was going on?

"I called you a dog and you didn't believe me? Then let me send you off to the next life!" Su Yang ignored the other four, eyes fixed only on the short man on the ground.

A flash of golden light, and a blurred afterimage streaked across the vision of the four Wang Manor experts.

A muffled grunt followed. By the time the four came back to their senses and looked again at the short man on the ground, all that remained was a smear of bloody pulp.

The youth before them was studying the four with a demonic expression, his voice cold enough to cut bone. "Next?"

Only now did the remaining four Wang Manor men finally understand. This youth was no soft target.

The bearded brute snapped out an order. "Spread out! Brace for combat!"

The other three sprang into motion at once, taking up three different angles to box Su Yang in.

"Brat, who in the world are you? You dare kill men of our Wang Manor? Do you have any idea you're walking yourself into the grave?" The bearded brute fixed Su Yang with a frigid stare.

"That's right. The men I'm killing are exactly your Wang Manor men." Su Yang did not rush to attack. He still had things he wanted to ask of these Wang Manor men.

"Oh? You do realize Yebei City is now under the rule of our Wang Manor, don't you? What faction are you from, brat? Care to give us a name?"

"Don't worry. You'll know soon enough."

"But I have a few questions of my own first. Answer them, and I might consider letting your corpses go intact."

"Hahaha! Amusing. You're nothing but a True Martial Realm cultivator, and you dare run your mouth in front of me? Kill him!" The bearded brute gave Su Yang no opening. Spiritual energy erupted from his body, condensing into a shield of qi that wrapped him from head to toe.

Three distinct streams of force surged from inside the bearded brute at once. Tri-Profound Power. The signature might of one who had stepped into the Tri-Profound Realm.

The other three True Martial Realm experts struck in unison. The only gap between True Martial and Tri-Profound was the absence of Tri-Profound Power within the body. All they could do was condense spiritual energy into a shield and fill every limb and bone with explosive strength.

"Blazing Fire Fist!"

"Wild Bull Force!"

"Wind God Kick!"

The three True Martial Realm experts unleashed their respective martial techniques, the sheer momentum of it sending nearby birds scattering into the sky.

The overwhelming spiritual energy churned into a whirlwind in the air, enough to set the heart pounding.

The bearded brute came in even harder. Years of combat had taught him one thing. If you fight, you finish your enemy in a single move. He showed no mercy and no longer cared what faction the youth belonged to. Kill first, ask later.

"Brat, I'm going to make sure you understand. You've crossed the wrong man."

"Let me show you how terrifying a Tri-Profound Realm expert truly is!"

"Tri-Profound Power, shaking mountains and rivers!"

"Now die!"

The bearded brute leapt high into the void, Tri-Profound qi coiling around him, every so often erupting with a staggering surge that poured into his body.

In that moment, the brute was like a giant elephant.

Savagery roared out of him as his mountainous frame came crashing down toward Su Yang in a brutal stomp.

Facing the combined assault of four enemies, Su Yang did not dare grow careless. He drew in a deep breath and shut his eyes, his mind echoing with every moment of these three months spent cultivating the Battle Sage Art.

The wooden staff was clenched tight in his hand. His Supreme Bloodline began to boil.

Then a golden radiance erupted from within Su Yang's body, a tremendous battle aura pouring out of him like a bursting spring.

In that instant Su Yang's eyes snapped open. His staff swept through the air as his whole body launched upward, golden light flashing around him. "Battle! Sage! Art!!!"

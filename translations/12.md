# Chapter 12: Taming the Mad Lion

The moment Su Yang stirred awake, the Great Sage's voice rolled out, mocking. "What? You scared of kicking the bucket again too? You really don't fear death, do you, kid? But this Great Sage approves! Hahaha!"

Su Yang scratched the back of his head sheepishly. "Heh-heh, I was just worried this little lion would book it into the deep mountains. Then I'd really be in trouble."

The Purple Lightning Mad Lion next to them looked mortified. "Master... from now on, I'm your sidekick."

At those words, Su Yang's eyes blew wide, and he jolted to his feet, staring hard at the Purple Lightning Mad Lion. "...You... what did you just call me?"

"M-Master..." The Purple Lightning Mad Lion hadn't expected the human youth in front of it to react this strongly. Its answer came out with a stammer.

"Kid, don't waste time. This Great Sage will pass on a Soul Contract technique to you. The moment you and it sign the contract, this little kitty will obey your every word." As the voice spoke, the method of the Soul Contract technique surfaced in Su Yang's mind in an instant.

Su Yang was over the moon at that. He knew the Purple Lightning Mad Lion's combat strength better than anyone. Without the Battle Sage Art, the lion would have killed him eight hundred times over.

"Heh-heh, little lion. I know you're not exactly thrilled about signing a contract with me. But the call isn't yours anymore. For your own neck's sake, do as Senior says."

"And as long as I, Su Yang, am still alive, I won't let you down."

"By my essence blood, the contract is sealed."

Su Yang wasn't one to dither. He'd said what needed saying. Whether the Purple Lightning Mad Lion would dare to turn on him later was up to the lion itself.

Su Yang bit open his fingertip, and a drop of essence blood sank into the Purple Lightning Mad Lion's skull in an instant.

"Roaaar—" The Purple Lightning Mad Lion's expression contorted as it let out a roar, as if some force were pulling its very soul out of its body.

Su Yang watched the lion's expression and reached out his right hand, pinching the air in front of him. A blue soul appeared instantly in his palm. It was a miniature Purple Lightning Mad Lion, standing motionless.

Su Yang let out a breath of relief at the sight. The contract had been signed.

The Purple Lightning Mad Lion returned to its former composure. But staring at its own soul lying in Su Yang's palm, it couldn't put the feeling into words.

*From now on, my fate is no longer mine to control. I can only hope this human youth in front of me really won't make me regret this...*

"All right. As you are now, it's time to prepare for your war of vengeance."

"Want to rest a day? Or shall we start training right now?" the Great Sage asked.

Su Yang's heart was already on fire. Subduing the Purple Lightning Mad Lion gave him more confidence, but his own strength was the only thing that mattered in the end. Either he'd fall and never rise, or he'd rise and shake the world!

"Let's start now. This junior is ready." Su Yang looked at the statue in front of him, his gaze rock-steady.

"Good! In that case, this Great Sage will train you for another ten days. If you can survive these ten days, you can leave the mountain and take your vengeance."

"If you can't, then you'll just keep training like a good kid until you've climbed back up to the Tri-Profound Realm."

The Purple Lightning Mad Lion stood frozen on the spot, dumbstruck, with no idea what their conversation was actually about.

Still, it could tell that the kid's monstrous edge had to come from this mysterious Senior's teaching.

At the thought, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion grinned and wagged its tail. "Senior, you see... could this junior maybe train a little too?"

Su Yang turned his gaze toward the Purple Lightning Mad Lion, his expression going strange.

As if to say, *You sure about that, dumbass?*

"Hahaha! I almost forgot about you. Since you're this kid's battle pet now, you obviously need to bring your strength up too. You're only mid rank-six right now, weak as hell. Train with this kid then. But let me be clear up front: if you can't stick with it, if you make this Great Sage unhappy, the consequences will be severe."

Hearing this, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion regretted running its mouth a moment ago.

Wasn't it just asking for trouble?

Watching its master's strange expression, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion already knew. This so-called training... was probably going to be a nightmare!

...

Meanwhile, in Yebei City.

Su Beilou and a handful of Su Family experts walked out of the Wang Manor, their faces grim as could be. Clearly they had taken a heavy blow.

Inside the Wang Manor, Wang Lang watched Su Beilou leave, calmly sipping his tea. "That Su Beilou really is impatient. He didn't even let this Patriarch finish before storming out."

"Patriarch Wang, if you'd said those words to this City Lord, I'm afraid this City Lord would have walked out on the spot." The City Lord, Li Beiye, who had stayed silent up to now, finally spoke. His tone was as unfriendly as it could be.

"Hahaha, City Lord Li, no need to panic. You and I are brothers. We can hardly be compared to the Su Family. The Su Family is finished as a force in this city. Rather than let them waste resources, why not let the two of us absorb them? Wouldn't that be ideal?" Wang Lang answered with a smile.

"Patriarch Wang, no need to beat around the bush. You absorb the Su Family this time. Next time, I suppose it'll be my City Lord's Manor that disappears?"

"Don't think that just because your Wang Family has two Divine Martial Realm cultivators, you can take all of Yebei City. This City Lord won't hand it over without a fight."

"Farewell!" Li Beiye said, and stormed out in fury.

Wang Lang watched Li Beiye's retreating back without making the slightest move to stop him. A thread of killing intent flashed in his eyes.

"Ungrateful fool!" Wang Yiyi snapped, her voice cold as ice.

"It seems Patriarch Wang has run into some trouble. If this Prince's help is needed, Patriarch Wang has only to ask." The Third Prince, off to the side, kept his smile in place as he spoke.

"For Your Highness to say so is reason enough for this Wang Lang to be content. But I beg Your Highness not to worry. A trifle like this, my Wang Family will handle on its own."

"Good. In that case, this Prince will be on his way. I'd ask Patriarch Wang to think over what my Imperial Father said. Within three days, give this Prince your answer."

At those words, Wang Lang lost the composure he'd had a moment before. He rose and bowed. "Your Highness may rest assured. Within three days, this Patriarch will deliver an answer to your satisfaction."

Having received the answer he wanted, the Third Prince left the Wang Manor as well.

That left only the Senior from the Moongaze Sect inside the Wang Manor.

"Patriarch Wang has impressive nerve. No surprise you raised a daughter as remarkable as Wang Yiyi." The Moongaze Sect Elder smiled.

"Elder Bi flatters me. My daughter is only a touch above ordinary. Set against your Moongaze Sect's prodigies, she still has a way to go. I hope, going forward, Elder Bi will look after her with extra care." Words like that proved just how cunning and calculating Wang Lang truly was.

"But of course. Wang Yiyi is exceptional, and more than that, she's a disciple personally chosen by our Sect Master. Her future will be boundless."

"However, this Elder has one more matter to ask of Patriarch Wang. May Patriarch Wang grant me an answer?"

"Oh? Elder Bi may speak freely."

"If this Elder isn't mistaken, your daughter carries two distinct bloodline powers, doesn't she?"

At those words, both Patriarch Wang and Wang Yiyi's expressions shifted at once.

They hadn't expected the Moongaze Sect Elder in front of them to see what was unusual about Wang Yiyi's body at a single glance.

"An Elder of Moongaze Sect indeed. What a keen eye. That's right. My daughter does carry two bloodlines!" Wang Lang answered cleanly and decisively. There was no need to hide a thing like this.

"So it is true?" Hearing this, Elder Bi's joyful expression grew even more intense, his excitement rising.

"It's true, Elder Bi. My body does carry two bloodline powers." Before Wang Lang could respond, Wang Yiyi had already cut in.

"Hahaha! Good, very good. To think our Moongaze Sect would have such a fortune."

"Wang Yiyi, time is of the essence. If there's nothing else holding you here, why not return to the Sect with this Elder within ten days?" The way Elder Bi was looking at Wang Yiyi now, you'd think she were a priceless treasure. His eyes wouldn't leave her for a second, and the excitement on his face was plain for anyone to see.

Wang Yiyi naturally had no reason to refuse.

Just as she was about to respond, a frantic voice burst through the doorway. "Patriarch! Patriarch... something terrible has happened. Steward Dao is dying!"

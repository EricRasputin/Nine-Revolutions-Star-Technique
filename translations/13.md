# Chapter 13: A Twist of Fate

"Hmph. What is it that has you in such a panic? It's not as if the sky is falling." A Wang Manor page came rushing into the main hall, frantic. With guests still present, Wang Lang's expression darkened.

He recovered quickly. "Steward Dao? What's going on? Didn't he take a few experts to the Demonic Beast Mountain Range?"

"P-Patriarch, this servant doesn't know what happened either. Steward Dao is at the gate now... he can't move, his breath's like a thread...." The page kept his head down, trembling all over.

At that, Wang Lang's brow furrowed.

He turned to the Moongaze Sect Elder with a smile. "Elder Bi, please take some rest first. This Patriarch will hold a proper welcoming banquet for you tonight. There's a family matter to attend to. I beg your understanding."

"Patriarch Wang is too courteous. Since your manor has matters to attend to, this old one won't impose." The Moongaze Sect Elder gave a faint smile.

"Yiyi, take Elder Bi to his quarters first."

"Yes."

With that, Wang Lang strode out of the hall, his face dark.

Before he could even reach the gate, the chatter of the crowd outside reached his ears.

"Tsk tsk, isn't that the Wang Manor's Steward Dao? How did he end up like this...."

"Who knows. By the look of him, he won't last long."

"Steward Dao is a Tri-Profound Realm Perfection expert, a name that rings out across Yebei City. Who could have wounded him this badly?"

"....Damned if I know. To pick a fight with the Wang Manor at a moment like this... that's no ordinary man."

"....."

The words made Wang Lang's expression darker still.

He wondered if his own men had run into that man-killing demonic beast, been overpowered, and limped home in defeat. It hadn't even occurred to him that another power in Yebei City might be behind it.

When Wang Lang stepped outside and saw Steward Dao lying on the ground at death's door, his face turned terrifyingly cold.

As a Divine Martial Realm expert, he could see at a glance that Steward Dao's wounds were plainly not the work of a beast. Beasts were not like men. Every strike of theirs left a trace, if only a residue of beast aura.

Yet there wasn't a trace of beast aura on Steward Dao. Instead... there lingered a fighting spirit so fierce it made even him feel a tremor of dread....

"Carry him into the manor, quickly." With that, Wang Lang turned on his heel and left.

The servants nearby moved at once. The page who had been greeting guests earlier knew his job well. "Friends and elders, our Wang Manor thanks you all for coming to offer your congratulations. Please disperse for now. The Wang Manor will repay your kindness another day."

With that, ignoring the crowd's reaction, he shut the gate.

Steward Dao was soon carried into a private chamber.

Wang Lang remained alone in the room. He looked at his subordinate, circulated the spiritual energy within his body, pressed several acupoints on Steward Dao in succession, then took out a pill and fed it into his mouth.

Steward Dao, who had been unconscious until then, slowly opened his eyes.

Wang Lang asked at once. "Who did this to you?"

Wang Lang had bought him one more breath, but Steward Dao's organs and Eight Extraordinary Meridians had all been shattered by Su Yang's overwhelming power. Even Wang Lang could not pull him back!

Steward Dao stared with eyes full of terror, his mouth working open and shut, producing only the faintest of sounds.

Wang Lang leaned in, putting his right ear close to listen.

"Patriarch.... Be... be... careful... Su... Su...." Before Steward Dao could finish, he could hold on no longer. His head lolled to one side, and he kicked the bucket.

That he had held on this long was thanks to a forbidden technique. Add to that the Purple Lightning Mad Lion having been nearby at the time, scaring off no end of demonic beasts. Only that had let Steward Dao make it back to the Wang Manor's gate at all.....

For him to manage even those few words at the end was already the last gasp of a man on his way out.

"Su? Su what?!" Wang Lang was beside himself. Of all the times for the man to die, couldn't he have at least finished his sentence first?

And now look at this. All he'd gotten out was "be careful of Su... something," and the man was gone.

*Could he have been telling me to watch out for the Su Family?*

The thought struck Wang Lang at once, and his face darkened. *Could it be that Su Beilou knew I meant to move against the Su Family, knew I'd sent Steward Dao and the others to the Demonic Beast Mountain Range, and dispatched his own experts to ambush them?*

*Damn him! Damn that Su Beilou!*

*If that's the way of it, I, Wang Lang, will make your Su Family pay this blood debt in blood!*

Wang Lang had already set his mind on wiping out the Su Family. Now he had the perfect pretext to act. When the time came, the Su Family would either bend the knee completely, or not a single one of them would be left alive.

At that moment, Wang Yiyi pushed open the door and stepped in.

When she saw Steward Dao already dead, her cold face tightened into a frown. "Father, what happened here?"

Seeing it was his daughter, Wang Lang's grim expression eased a little. "Hmph. The Su Family had the gall to strike at our Wang Manor first. Has Su Beilou forgotten his own measure?"

"The Su Family? You're saying it was the Su Family that did this to Steward Dao?" Wang Yiyi sounded doubtful.

"Mm. Just before he died, Steward Dao told your father here to be careful of Su... something. The last word never came out, but you don't need to think twice. He was telling this Patriarch to watch out for the Su Family." Wang Lang's tone was firm.

At that, Wang Yiyi fell silent.

As she looked at the dead Steward Dao, an uneasy premonition rose in her chest.

*Su... could it be him? No, that's impossible! In the state he was in, even if he didn't die, there's no way he could have recovered in three short months, let alone defeat a Steward Dao at Tri-Profound Realm Perfection.*

*It must be as Father says. The Su Family is behind it.*

*And yet.... is that really what's going on here?*

A flicker of unease ran through Wang Yiyi, but there was nothing more she could guess at for now.

"Hmph. We'll keep this debt on the books for the moment. Once three days have passed and your matter with the Third Prince is settled, that's the day we'll settle accounts with the Su Family."

"Yiyi, the Third Prince is a fine young man. He has a constitution beyond the ordinary, and they say he's a strong contender for the Crown Prince's seat. You may carry two bloodlines, and the Moongaze Sect's Sect Master may have set her sights on you, but if you want your road ahead to be free of worry, marrying into the Great Xia Dynasty is the better path."

"What do you think?" Wang Lang looked at his daughter, his tone gentle.

Wang Yiyi was silent a moment, then raised her head. "Father, this daughter has no wish to marry into the imperial family so soon. His Highness the Third Prince is fine, but this daughter's achievements in the years to come will surely surpass his. By that day, not even the Great Xia Dynasty will dare touch a single hair of our Wang Family."

"I ask Father to understand this daughter's decision."

Wang Yiyi met Wang Lang's eyes, her resolve unshaken.

Wang Lang seemed to have expected as much, and could only sigh. "Very well. If that's how it is, we'll set the marriage aside for now. When the time comes, I'll have Elder Bi accompany me to explain things to His Highness the Third Prince. I'm sure the Xia Emperor won't make trouble for us over this!"

"Let's settle it that way. For the next few days, rest properly. Once everything here is dealt with, you can spread your wings and soar!"

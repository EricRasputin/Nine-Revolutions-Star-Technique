# Chapter 4: The Battle Sage Art!

Inside a stone cave in the Demonic Beast Mountain Range.

Su Yang sat cross-legged, using his divine sense to commune with the stone.

"Senior Great Sage, you want me to enter the stone?" Su Yang stared at the black stone hanging from his chest, genuinely baffled.

"That's right. Don't assume this is just some ordinary rock. Send your divine sense in, and I guarantee you'll be shocked." The stone replied.

"Alright. I'll send it in now."

Without further hesitation, Su Yang extended his divine sense toward the black stone.

The instant it made contact, a tremendous suction force surged outward. Before Su Yang could even react, his entire body vanished from the cave. All that remained was a black stone, lying quietly on the ground.

The dizziness faded gradually. Su Yang opened his eyes.

Before him stretched an endless starfield, a sky packed with celestial bodies and meteorites of every size.

Su Yang stood there with his mouth hanging open and his eyes blown wide, utterly dumbstruck. He had never once imagined that inside this tiny stone lay an entire cosmos, an entire universe of stars.

"Th-this place is..."

He stammered, struggling to form a question.

"Not a bad-looking kid, I'll admit. This Great Sage is over here." The familiar voice rang out. Su Yang turned toward it.

Not far away, atop a conspicuously massive meteorite, he could make out what appeared to be a figure.

Su Yang squinted and called out: "Senior Great Sage, is that you?"

"Hmph! Who else would be here? In this godforsaken place, there's no one but me." The voice carried a hint of irritation.

Su Yang scrambled toward the massive meteorite without a second thought.

He reached the top and went still all over again.

A humanoid stone statue stood at the dead center of the meteorite.

And above it, an enormous stone palm pressed down upon it, pinning the statue in place.

A strange sight unlike anything Su Yang had ever encountered.

"Stop staring. That statue is this Great Sage." The voice came again. Su Yang moved to the statue's side and studied it carefully for a long moment.

He noticed the statue was not entirely human in appearance. The face, at least, had a pointed chin and sunken cheeks, like a monkey's face.

"You are... the Senior Great Sage?" Su Yang reached out to poke the statue, wanting to test whether it was real. His finger never made contact. A violent force erupted from the statue's surface and flung him back several zhang, blood running from the corner of his mouth.

"Touch me without permission again, kid, and you die."

"This is no ordinary place. See that stone palm above your head? If that thing releases its power, it will erase you in an instant."

Su Yang shuddered, suddenly very grateful he had not pressed further.

"Kid, don't waste time with unnecessary questions. What you need to know, this Great Sage will tell you in time. For now, worry about yourself. You were born with the Supreme Bloodline — your future should have been limitless. Three years ago you stood in the Tri-Profound Realm. Now you're a cripple. Your dantian is empty. Vengeance is not something you'll achieve in a day."

"That girl carries the Fire Phoenix Bloodline. If she truly manages to fuse it with your Supreme Bloodline, she will become a terrifying existence."

"Your path to revenge only grows harder from here."

The Great Sage's voice fell quiet.

Su Yang took the words in. He knew how weak he was, and he knew this was no time to pry into matters beyond his concern. He turned toward the statue and bowed with deep sincerity: "If the Senior Great Sage has summoned this junior here, then surely there must be a way to help me defy what fate has dealt me."

"I beg you, Senior. If there is even a sliver of hope, this junior is willing to try."

"Hmm. Your resolve is commendable. Recovering to the Tri-Profound Realm in a short time is essentially out of the question. As you can see, this Great Sage is nothing more than a stone statue. There is not much I can do directly."

"However, this Great Sage possesses one method that can raise your combat strength in a short time without requiring a single wisp of spiritual energy. There is a catch: in a thousand years, no one besides this Great Sage has ever been able to see this method through. And once you begin training in it, you must go all the way. Abandon it halfway, and you will be reduced to a cripple beyond all recovery."

"Do you dare try?"

Su Yang listened to every word with absolute focus.

The moment the voice fell silent, he answered without hesitation: "This junior is willing."

"Ha ha ha! Kid, you had better think this through. This method of mine struck terror into all who heard its name even a thousand years ago. Supreme Bloodline or not, you may not be able to endure it."

"This Great Sage asks you one more time. Do you dare try?"

Su Yang straightened his back and stepped toward the statue: "What is there to fear? Look at the state of me. Even with the Supreme Bloodline, returning to my peak is a distant dream. As you said, this junior's path to vengeance will not be easy. If it means growing stronger — no matter how bitter, no matter how grueling, even if I must walk a razor's edge — this junior is willing. No regrets."

Su Yang finished speaking, and the statue said nothing.

A long pause. Then a booming laugh broke from the stone: "Ha ha ha! Good! Not bad at all, kid. You've got the same fire this Great Sage had in his younger days."

"Very well. I'll let you give this method a try."

"Come to my side."

Su Yang did not hesitate. He walked straight to the statue.

"Kneel!"

Without a second's pause, Su Yang dropped to both knees with a thud.

"I'll spare you the formal kowtow. From this day forward, you are this Great Sage's disciple. Today I pass on to you my combat art — the Battle Sage Art. To cultivate this technique requires no spiritual energy. Only your body, your willpower, and your flesh and blood, forged through relentless combat to temper your strength."

"Now, this Great Sage will engrave the cultivation method of the Battle Sage Art directly into your mind. Starting today, you will train here for twenty hours each day and may not leave before those twenty hours are up. Can you do it?"

Su Yang felt like his skull was splitting open. Image after image poured into his mind like a rushing spring.

Cold sweat soaked through him. His teeth ground together so hard they creaked.

At last, bracing himself on the ground with both hands and gasping for breath, he forced out his answer: "Your disciple... will see it done."

"Good. Rest up first. We begin shortly."

"The Battle Sage Art holds no rank or classification. It is a combat method devised solely by this Great Sage. Only through my direct instruction can you master its foundations in the shortest time."

"If you can endure it, within four months this Great Sage will grant you combat power rivaling the Divine Martial Realm."

A spark lit in Su Yang's eyes — joy and hunger burning bright together.

"Senior Great Sage, I am ready."

"Begin now. And don't hold back."

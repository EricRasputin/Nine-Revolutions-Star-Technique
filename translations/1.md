# Chapter 1: Three Years a Blood Slave

The Eastern Xuan Continent.

Within Yebei City!

Inside a secret chamber of the Wang estate, a woman was pushing open a stone door. It groaned with a heavy click before slowly grinding aside.

The scene that greeted her eyes was a skeletal young man, bound fast by several black iron chains, locked tight against the wall behind the door.

The youth's face was deathly pale, utterly drained of color, and only the faintest whisper of breath proved he was still alive.

The woman walked toward him with light, graceful steps, her figure elegant and poised.

"Su Yang, today is the last time I'll be taking your blood. I have to admit, you held on longer than I expected — it took a full three years to drain every last drop of Supreme Blood from your body. You really have suffered for me." The woman regarded the frail youth before her, her voice cold beyond measure.

"Heh heh... heh heh!"

"Wang... Yiyi, even if you drain every drop of Supreme Blood from my body... so what? That bloodline will never truly be yours. I, Su Yang, will never let you go — not even in death!" The youth's name was Su Yang, formerly the Young Master of the Su Family in Yebei City, once the pride of his clan and the most celebrated prodigy the city had ever known. Born with the Supreme Bloodline, he had entered the Spirit Transformation Realm at the age of three, reached the True Martial Realm by five, and stepped into the Triple Profound Realm at just ten years old.

With such a dazzling record, everyone believed Su Yang was destined to become the first cultivator in Yebei City's history to break through to the Divine Martial Realm before the age of fifteen.

Yet a single marriage had made Su Yang vanish from the world entirely.

Yebei City had long been governed by three great powers: the City Lord's Manor, the Su Family, and the Wang Family. The three factions had coexisted in peace — until Su Yang's birth. The emergence of the Supreme Bloodline drove both the City Lord's Manor and the Wang Family to seek a marriage alliance with the Su clan.

When Su Yang turned twelve, the three families sat down together and let him choose his future bride, with the engagement to be formalized immediately and the wedding held after he turned fifteen.

No one could say which eye Su Yang had been blind in when he chose the Wang Family's daughter — the very woman standing in this chamber now — Wang Yiyi.

It was not until the Wang Family conspired to trap him, binding him with eight Soul-Suppressing Iron Chains and sealing him inside this secret chamber, that Su Yang finally understood: the so-called marriage alliance had been nothing but an elaborate scheme from the very beginning.

The Supreme Bloodline was rare beyond measure. Wang Yiyi herself carried the Fire Phoenix Bloodline, and if it could be fused with the Supreme Bloodline until the two became one, the Wang Family would inevitably produce a supreme powerhouse. At that point, neither the City Lord's Manor nor the Su Family would be worth fearing.

And so, for three long years, Wang Yiyi had come to the chamber once every three days to collect a vial of Su Yang's Supreme Blood.

Three years. Su Yang could no longer remember how he had survived them.

As for the Su Family, Su Yang's disappearance had plunged them into civil war. His parents were framed by the Grand Elder — a man who had long coveted the position of family head — and exiled to the hinterlands. Everyone connected to Su Yang was reassigned or removed, and those who dared resist were struck down publicly by the Grand Elder as a warning to all.

From that day forward, the Su Family fell into ruin, puppet strings firmly in the Grand Elder's hands, and through him, in the Wang Family's grasp.

"Su Yang, rest easy. I, Wang Yiyi, am not some ungrateful wretch. All these years, I've had you to thank — you and that bloodline of yours. Thanks to you, I've reached the Perfection stage of the Triple Profound Realm in one leap. After I take your blood just one more time today, I'll be ready to assault the Divine Martial Realm."

"When the time comes, I'll be sure to thank you properly."

"Hmph!"

Even as she spoke, the woman drew a gleaming little knife and drove it mercilessly into skin so wasted there was scarcely any flesh left to cut.

Golden blood began to seep out, drop by agonizing drop, impossibly slow...

She produced a jade vial and pressed it urgently against the wound. As the golden blood dripped into the vial, excitement crept unbidden across her face.

Just one more step. Just this one small vial. One final absorption of Supreme Blood, and she would become the most dazzling prodigy Yebei City had ever seen!

Su Yang could no longer feel the pain. That sensation had become something he was long accustomed to, and there was not a shred of strength left in his entire body.

His dantian had long since run dry. Not a wisp of spiritual energy remained... Within his Eight Extraordinary Meridians, only the barest trickle of blood still flowed...

It was hard to imagine that in a body so ravaged, Su Yang still had the strength to speak.

Watching the greed written across Wang Yiyi's face, Su Yang could not help but laugh: "Heh... heh heh, ha ha ha!" The laughter was faint, yet it carried with perfect clarity to Wang Yiyi's ears.

Wang Yiyi frowned, unable to fathom why a man on death's doorstep would be laughing.

Had he lost his mind entirely?

"Wang Yiyi, I'll give you that — you're ruthless. To think that I, Su Yang, the prodigy of the Su Family, would be taken in by your honeyed words. You didn't just deceive me — you stole my Supreme Blood and penned me up like a blood slave."

"You! What a vicious heart you have."

Su Yang raised his gaze. In those hollow, lightless pupils, a surge of killing intent — fiercer than anything he had ever felt — erupted without warning, locking onto Wang Yiyi with lethal intensity.

Wang Yiyi, who had been extracting his blood with painstaking care, flinched at his stare. Her delicate hand trembled, and a single drop of Supreme Blood fell to the ground...

She had never expected Su Yang to still be capable of such a terrifying, murderous gaze. A chill of fear rose unbidden in her chest. But the feeling vanished just as quickly — what a joke. He was already a cripple. Once she finished draining this last batch of blood, he would be dead. Why should she fear a dead man?

Still, the wasted drop of Supreme Blood left Wang Yiyi seething. Her already frigid expression turned glacial as she spoke: "Su Yang, spare me that look. The path of cultivation has always been cruel. You simply deserved it. Who told you to trust me so blindly back then? Truly laughable."

"Heh heh, yes. Truly laughable indeed."

"Laughable that I was so blind. Laughable that I never saw through your Wang Family's plot sooner. Laughable that I actually believed your words, and let myself be trapped so effortlessly."

"Wang Yiyi, mark my words. If I, Su Yang, do not die today, you had best never close your eyes to sleep — because I will leave your Wang Family in utter ruin, every last one of you cut down to the root!" Su Yang glared at the venomous woman before him, biting off each word through clenched teeth. The fury in his eyes blazed hot enough to ignite twin infernos and burn her to ash.

"Enough with the empty threats. Save your breath. Rest assured, I won't let you die easily. Even after every drop of Supreme Blood has been drained from your body, I will see to it that you end this life in the most agonizing way possible."

Wang Yiyi's tone remained ice-cold. Su Yang said nothing more, only lowered his head in silence. The furious light in his eyes had faded, replaced once more by hollow emptiness...

"Hm? There's not a single drop of Supreme Blood left?" Wang Yiyi stared at the wound — not one more drop of golden blood flowed from it — and frowned.

She shook the jade vial in her hand, and her brow furrowed deeper still.

She glanced at Su Yang's bowed head, then snatched up the knife again and stabbed him several more times in quick succession.

Su Yang did not stir, as though he were already dead.

From the fresh wounds, not a single drop of Supreme Blood emerged.

Wang Yiyi surveyed the scene, her frost-cold expression growing even icier: "How infuriating — only half a vial. And I wasted a drop earlier, you worthless wretch!"

Whether Su Yang heard her or not was unclear. Wang Yiyi lifted his skull-like face with one hand, seeming to want to say something more, but his tightly shut eyes made her swallow the words. She released him and rose to her feet.

At that moment, another figure stepped through the stone doorway.

"Yiyi, how did it go? Was this final extraction a success?" The newcomer's voice boomed. He appeared to be in his forties or fifties, with a square jaw, a hooked nose, and eyes that flickered with a barely perceptible cunning.

"Father, not entirely. There isn't a single drop of Supreme Blood left in Su Yang's body. This time, I only managed half a vial." Wang Yiyi offered a slight bow upon seeing the arrival.

"Oh? Only half a vial. What a pity." The man furrowed his brows, genuine regret in his tone.

This was none other than the Wang Family's patriarch — Wang Lang! One of Yebei City's three greatest experts, a cultivator of the Divine Martial Realm.

"No matter. By now, your Fire Phoenix Bloodline and the Supreme Bloodline have nearly finished merging. Even half a vial should be enough for you to break through to the Divine Martial Realm. Come — it's time to put an end to all of this." Wang Lang regarded his daughter, his voice calm and measured.

But behind his eyes, visions of a grand future flickered and gleamed.

As long as his daughter could forge the Supreme Fire Phoenix Bloodline and step into the Divine Martial Realm, the Wang Family would one day claim a seat of power across the entire Eastern Xuan Continent!

"Mm, it shouldn't be a problem."

"As for Su Yang..." Wang Yiyi seemed to be seeking her father's opinion.

"The Su Family already believes he's dead. He's nothing but a cripple now anyway. It wouldn't do to let him die on Wang Family grounds — after all, his father and I were once on good terms. And he was engaged to you. Without his sacrifice, you wouldn't be who you are today."

"I'll have someone toss him into the Demonic Beast Mountain Range shortly." Wang Lang cast one final glance at Su Yang, still bound in iron chains, and the corner of his mouth curled upward.

"I leave everything to Father's judgment. However, your daughter has one more request."

"Oh? What would that be?"

Wang Yiyi turned around and glanced once more at Su Yang, half-kneeling on the ground. Remembering the terrifying look he had fixed on her moments ago, she turned away and spoke in a low, ghostly murmur: "I want to sever the tendons in his hands and feet..."

# Chapter 15: The Wang Family Strikes, the Su Family in Peril

At that moment, outside the Wang Manor.

A group of riders sat mounted on demonic beasts at the manor gate.

Wang Lang and Wang Yiyi stood at the gate, smiling and seeing them off with the utmost deference.

"Safe travels, Your Highness. If our hospitality fell short in any way, I beg the Third Prince not to take it to heart." Wang Lang stood beside a rank-five demonic beast, a Blazing Flame Horse, and bowed his head in salute.

"Patriarch Wang need not stand on ceremony. This prince only came to convey His Majesty's decree. In any case, this prince hopes that in time the Wang Family will become a great pillar of our Great Xia Dynasty, and that Lady Yiyi will rise to renown within the Moongaze Sect." The Third Prince said it with his smile still in place.

"Your Highness flatters me. Yiyi will cultivate diligently." Wang Yiyi gave a small bow of thanks.

"Then so be it. This prince takes his leave."

With that, the Third Prince and his retinue mounted up and rode their demonic beasts out beyond Yebei City.

Watching the Third Prince's party disappear into the distance, Wang Lang's expression turned grave, for he had gone against the Xia Emperor's wishes, and if His Majesty were truly displeased, the consequences would be severe.

The only play left was to cling tight to the Moongaze Sect's coattails.

Only after the Third Prince's party had fully cleared Yebei City did Wang Lang lead the manor's people back inside.

Not far away, inside the Su Family's main hall.

"Reporting to the patriarch, the Third Prince has left the city. That Moongaze Sect elder has not shown himself in some time either." A Su Family expert knelt before Su Beilou and made his report.

Su Beilou had grown haggard of late, his face years older than it had been three days ago.

"Understood. Keep your men on the Wang Family, and the moment anything stirs, report to me at once." Su Beilou waved his hand, his tone edged with impatience.

"Yes! As you command."

At his word, the Su Family expert was gone in an instant.

Just then, a figure appeared at Su Beilou's side. "Father, what is there to be so anxious about? If you ask me, the Wang Family won't necessarily move against us. We were the ones who helped Wang Lang drive Su Yang's parents out of the family back then, so would he really go back on his word now?"

He was strikingly young, looking just past twenty.

Beneath shoulder-length black hair was a strikingly handsome face, his expression touched with arrogance, as though he held no fear of the Wang Family at all.

He was the Su Family's current young master, Su Beilou's own son: Su Shaofeng!

Hearing his son speak, Su Beilou could only manage a bitter smile. "Shaofeng, you're still too young: the Wang Family's power has long since outstripped ours, especially with that daughter of theirs now stepping into the Divine Martial Realm, and with the Great Xia Dynasty and the Moongaze Sect at her back, you really think Wang Lang has us in his sights at all? Even if you threw in the City Lord's Manor, I doubt he'd flinch."

At those words, Su Shaofeng's expression turned ugly.

He was barely past twenty, with peak Tri-Profound Realm cultivation, yet it had never crossed his mind that the Su Family might one day fall to the Wang Family. Back then, his cousin Su Yang had always overshadowed him.

He had assumed that with Su Yang gone, he would step into that place as Yebei City's golden child.

Who could have guessed Wang Yiyi would break into the Divine Martial Realm before him? It galled the proud Su Shaofeng to no end.

In his eyes, he was the strongest young master in the Su Family, the brightest star in Yebei City.

"Hmph! If Wang Lang truly dares to move on the Su Family, then even in death, I, Su Shaofeng, will sink my teeth in and tear a piece of flesh out of him." Su Shaofeng's fists clenched tight.

Hearing his son's resolve, Su Beilou felt a flicker of satisfaction.

But this was his only son, and if the Wang Family truly came for the Su Family they would salt the earth, so no matter what, he could not let his son come to harm.

Su Beilou thought it over, then said, "Shaofeng, your talent may not match Wang Yiyi's, but if you commit yourself to cultivation, your future won't fall short. In a moment, I'll have your second uncle take you out of Yebei City to stay with an old friend of mine for a while, and then we'll see about getting you into the Profound Heaven Sect."

"Father! You want me to play the deserter?" Su Shaofeng frowned, baffled.

"You foolish boy, your old man is trying to save your life. If you don't leave today, there's no telling when the Wang Family will come kicking down our door!" Su Beilou snapped, his hand rising to knock his son out, when a hearty burst of laughter rolled in from outside.

"Hahaha! Brother Beilou, I've come to pay you a visit!"

Boom!

A surge of overwhelming force came hurtling in from outside, and a body crashed clean through the door and landed at Su Beilou's feet like a dead dog.

At the sight, Su Beilou's expression changed and he shot to his feet, barking, "Wang Lang, what is the meaning of this?"

On those words, Wang Lang strode into the hall with several Wang Family experts at his back, Wang Yiyi and the Moongaze Sect elder among them.

At that sight, Su Beilou's face fell into outright despair.

Because he knew that it was already too late, that the Wang Family had come to put every last one of them in the ground!

Seeing how tense Su Beilou was, Wang Lang chuckled. "Brother Beilou, what's there to be so tense about? I only came for an explanation."

"An explanation? What explanation? Hmph! Spit it out, then, and don't think I can't see what you've got cooking in there." Su Beilou stared at Wang Lang, baffled, unable to make out what he was really up to.[^1]

Now that the Wang Family had really come, with the patriarch himself leading the way, Su Shaofeng's earlier swagger drained away in an instant, replaced by a touch of regret.

*If only I'd left a little sooner.*

But Su Shaofeng put on a hard face anyway. "Uncle Wang, this doesn't look to me like a visit for explanations. You came for blood, didn't you?"

"Aha-ha, nephew[^2], listen to yourself. I, Wang Lang, watched you grow up from a bare-assed kid running around the manor, so how could I be here to take lives? It's just that your Uncle Wang has his hands tied here, and the Su Family hasn't played fair." Wang Lang shook his head over and over.

"Oh? Why's that? In my own view, this Su has done nothing of late to provoke the Wang Family." Su Beilou's eyes went wide.

"Brother Beilou, first take a look at the body at your feet. Doesn't he look familiar?"

Su Beilou looked down, a pulse of spiritual energy flipping the body face-up, and once he saw the features he asked in greater confusion, "Isn't this your Wang Family's Steward Dao? What's going on? What is this?"

"So Brother Beilou wants to play the fool? Fine, since that's how it is, I won't dance around it. Three days ago, I sent Steward Dao and several manor experts into the Demonic Beast Mountain Range to put down the beast that's been wounding people, but that very night, Steward Dao came staggering back gravely wounded."

"And he warned this patriarch to watch his back, to watch out for your Su Family!" Wang Lang's tone shifted, killing intent leaking from his eyes.

At those words, Su Beilou's heart sank, and he erupted. "You damned Wang Lang, if you mean to swallow the Su Family whole, just say it to my face, why frame us with someone else's blood? The Su Family is no lap dog of the Wang Family, and we won't be kneaded like dough in your hands!"

"What I, Su Beilou, regret most in this life is ever taking your honeyed lies at face value, which is what has brought the Su Family to this!"

"Wang Lang, don't think I'm afraid of you! Even if I have to die for it, I'll make your Wang Family pay a price you'll feel for generations!"

"Hahaha, what a joke. You, Su Beilou? You dare bark at me, Wang Lang, to my face?"

Wang Lang threw back his head and laughed, and at once a tremendous presence came roaring out of him.

[^1]: 葫芦里卖什么药 (hú lú lǐ mài shén me yào) — literally "what medicine is he selling out of his gourd?" An old Chinese idiom for "what's he scheming, what's the angle?", drawn from the picture of an itinerant medicine-seller whose closed gourd hides his real wares. Su Beilou can see Wang Lang's pitch but not the merchandise; the gourd doesn't survive in English, but the suspicion does.
[^2]: 大侄子 (dà zhí zi) — literally "big nephew." In older Chinese social register, an elder man addresses a younger family friend's child this way to flatter the bond and assert generational seniority. There is no actual blood tie. The 大 ("big") softens it further into something familiar and avuncular. Wang Lang reaches for it precisely because he is here to threaten the boy's life: putting on the mantle of beloved uncle while drawing the knife.

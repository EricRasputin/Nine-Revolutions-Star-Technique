# Chapter 15: The Wang Family Strikes, the Su Family in Peril

At that moment, outside the Wang Manor.

A column of riders sat astride several demonic beasts at the manor gate.

Wang Lang and Wang Yiyi stood at the gate, smiling them off with the utmost deference.

"Safe travels, Your Highness. If our hospitality fell short in any way, I beg the Third Prince not to take it amiss." Wang Lang stood beside a rank-five demonic beast — a Blazing Flame Horse — and bowed his head in salute.

"Patriarch Wang need not stand on ceremony. This prince[^1] only came to convey His Majesty's decree. Whatever else, I hope the Wang Family will one day become a great pillar of our Great Xia Dynasty, and that Lady Yiyi makes her name ring out across the Moongaze Sect." The Third Prince said it still wearing his smile.

"Your Highness flatters me. Yiyi will cultivate diligently." Wang Yiyi gave a small bow of thanks.

"Then so be it. This prince takes his leave."

With that, the Third Prince and his retinue mounted up and rode their demonic beasts out beyond Yebei City.

Watching the Third Prince's party disappear into the distance, Wang Lang's expression turned grave. He had, after all, gone against the Xia Emperor's wishes. If His Majesty were truly displeased, the consequences would be severe.

The only play left was to cling tight to the Moongaze Sect's coattails.

Only after the Third Prince's party had fully cleared Yebei City did Wang Lang lead the manor's people back inside.

Not far away, inside the Su Family's main hall.

"Reporting to the patriarch, the Third Prince has left the city. That Moongaze Sect elder has not shown himself in some time either." A Su Family expert knelt before Su Beilou and made his report.

Su Beilou had grown haggard of late. His face looked years older than it had three days ago.

"Understood. Keep men on the Wang Family. The moment anything stirs, report to me at once." Su Beilou waved a hand, his tone edged with impatience.

"Yes! As you command."

The Su Family expert acknowledged the order and was gone in an instant.

"Father, why so anxious? In my view, the Wang Family won't necessarily move against us. We were the ones who helped Wang Lang back then — drove Su Yang's parents out of the family for him. Would he really turn on his own word?" Just then, a figure appeared at Su Beilou's side.

He was strikingly young — barely past twenty by the look of him.

Beneath shoulder-length black hair, his face was strikingly handsome, his expression carrying a streak of pride. He didn't seem to fear the Wang Family in the slightest.

He was the Su Family's current young master, Su Beilou's own son: Su Shaofeng!

Hearing his son speak, Su Beilou could only manage a bitter smile. "Shaofeng, you're still too young. The Wang Family's power has long since outstripped ours. That daughter of theirs especially. She's stepped into the Divine Martial Realm, and she has the Great Xia Dynasty and the Moongaze Sect at her back. You think Wang Lang still has us in his sights at all? Even if you threw in the City Lord's Manor, he wouldn't flinch."

At those words, Su Shaofeng's expression turned ugly.

He was just past twenty, with a peak Tri-Profound Realm cultivation base, and it had never once crossed his mind that the Su Family might one day fall to the Wang Family. Back in the day, his cousin Su Yang had always overshadowed him.

He had assumed that with Su Yang gone, he would step into that place as Yebei City's golden child.

Who could have guessed Wang Yiyi would break into the Divine Martial Realm before him? It galled the proud Su Shaofeng to no end.

In his own eyes, he was the strongest young master the Su Family had — the brightest star Yebei City could boast.

"Hmph! If Wang Lang really dares to move on the Su Family, then I, Su Shaofeng, will tear a chunk off him with my teeth before I die." Su Shaofeng's fists clenched tight.

Watching his son show that kind of resolve, Su Beilou was satisfied enough.

But he had only the one son. If the Wang Family truly came for the Su Family, they would cut down every last root. No matter what, he could not let his son come to harm.

Su Beilou thought it over, then said, "Shaofeng. Your talent may not match Wang Yiyi's, but if you commit yourself to cultivation, your future won't fall short. In a moment I'll have your second uncle take you out of Yebei City — go stay with an old friend of mine for a while. See if you can get yourself into the Profound Heaven Sect."

"Father! Are you telling me to run?" Su Shaofeng frowned, uncomprehending.

"You foolish boy — your old man is trying to save your life. If you don't leave today, there's no telling when the Wang Family will come kicking down our door!" Su Beilou snapped, his hand rising to knock his son out, when a hearty burst of laughter rolled in from outside.

"Hahaha! Brother Beilou, this Wang has come to call!"

Boom!

A surge of overwhelming force came hurtling in from outside, and a body crashed clean through the door and landed at Su Beilou's feet like a dead dog.

Su Beilou's expression changed at the sight. He shot to his feet and barked, "Wang Lang, what is the meaning of this?"

On those words, Wang Lang strode into the hall with several Wang Family experts at his back, Wang Yiyi and the Moongaze Sect elder among them.

At that sight, Su Beilou's face fell into outright despair.

Because he knew it was already too late. The Wang Family had come to put every last one of them in the ground!

Seeing how tense Su Beilou was, Wang Lang chuckled. "Brother Beilou, why so wound up? This Wang only came for an explanation."

"An explanation? What explanation? Hmph! Out with it, then. Don't think I can't see what you've got cooking in there." Su Beilou stared at Wang Lang, baffled, unable to make out what Wang Lang was really up to.

Seeing the Wang Family had actually come, and the patriarch himself leading the way, Su Shaofeng felt all his earlier bluster evaporate, replaced by a flicker of regret.

*If only I'd left a little sooner.*

But Su Shaofeng forced his face hard anyway. "Uncle Wang, looks to me like you didn't come for an explanation — you came for blood, didn't you?"

"Aha-ha, nephew, listen to yourself. This Wang Lang watched you run around bare-bottomed as a boy. How could I have come for blood? It's just that your Uncle Wang has his hands tied here. The Su Family hasn't played fair." Wang Lang shook his head over and over.

"Oh? How so? This Su has done nothing of late to provoke the Wang Family — or so I thought." Su Beilou's eyes went wide.

"Brother Beilou, first take a look at the body at your feet. Doesn't he look familiar?"

Su Beilou looked down, and a pulse of spiritual energy turned the body face-up. Once he saw the features, he was more confused than before. "Isn't this your Wang Family's Steward Dao? What's going on? What is this?"

"So Brother Beilou wants to play the fool. Fine. In that case, this Wang Lang won't dance around it. Three days ago I sent Steward Dao and several manor experts into the Demonic Beast Mountain Range to put down the beast that's been wounding people. That very night, Steward Dao came staggering back gravely wounded."

"And he warned this patriarch[^2] to watch his back — to watch out for your Su Family!" Wang Lang's tone shifted, killing intent leaking from his eyes.

At those words, Su Beilou's heart sank, and he erupted. "You bastard, Wang Lang! If you mean to swallow the Su Family whole, say it to my face. Why frame us with someone else's blood? The Su Family is no lap dog of the Wang Family — and we won't be kneaded like dough in your hands!"

"The thing I, Su Beilou, regret most in this life is ever taking your honeyed lies at face value. That's what's brought the Su Family to this!"

"Wang Lang, don't think I'm afraid of you! Even if I have to die for it, I'll make your Wang Family pay a price you'll feel for generations!"

"Hahaha. What a joke. You, Su Beilou? You dare bark at me, Wang Lang, to my face?"

Wang Lang threw his head back in laughter, and at once a tremendous presence came roaring out of him.

---

[^1]: 本皇子 (běn huáng zǐ) — literally "this prince." A formal third-person self-reference used by an imperial son when speaking down to subjects, expressing both his rank and the distance between them. Like 本家主 ("this patriarch," used by Wang Lang later in the chapter), it sounds chilly and dignified in Chinese; English has no equivalent register, so the literal "this prince" is preserved to mark the formality.
[^2]: 本家主 (běn jiā zhǔ) — literally "this patriarch / this family-head." A formal self-reference Wang Lang uses to assert his authority as head of the Wang Family. Reaching for it in an accusation — "warned this patriarch to watch his back" — has the flavor of a man pulling rank mid-threat: positioning himself as the wronged great lord rather than a private person in a dispute.

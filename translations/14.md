# Chapter 14: Tri-Profound Breakthrough, Su Yang Descends the Mountain

Time passed. Three days later.

Within the stone world, deep in the Demonic Beast Mountain Range.

Beneath a sky full of stars, Su Yang and the Purple Lightning Mad Lion traded blows back and forth.

Boom! Boom! Boom!

Stunning surges of spiritual energy erupted again and again, deafening.

*This kid really is making astonishing progress. In just three short days, he can already pressure the Purple Lightning Mad Lion. It seems this Great Sage has underestimated his grit. The boy's future has no limit.* The Great Sage could feel the clash of auras between Su Yang and the Purple Lightning Mad Lion, and sighed inwardly.

The Great Sage wasn't the only one shocked at Su Yang's growth. The Purple Lightning Mad Lion, sparring with him at this very moment, was even more shaken.

Over just three short days of training alongside Su Yang, it had witnessed firsthand the terrifying art the human youth practiced: a fighting spirit that nothing could stand against.

It was downright off the charts.

On the Great Sage's instruction, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion had also taken up a powerful body art that would push its physical strength up another level.

But when it watched Su Yang relying on raw physical strength alone to ram himself again and again into the meteorites that filled the sky, its scalp prickled with horror.

Several times it had seen Su Yang nearly broken to pieces by the sheer force of those collisions.

And every time the Purple Lightning Mad Lion thought Su Yang couldn't take any more, he would drag himself to his feet and throw himself at the meteorites again, heedless of everything.

That kind of will genuinely commanded the Purple Lightning Mad Lion's awe.

As for itself, it had grown plenty in those three days too. Under Senior Great Sage's personal instruction, its physical strength had risen considerably, and even its realm had begun showing signs of breakthrough. The Purple Lightning Mad Lion was beside itself with joy.

It had thought its own progress was fast enough. But when Senior Great Sage had it spar with Su Yang today, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion finally understood: the so-called clown was itself.....

The Su Yang standing before it now was not the same person from three days ago. Three days ago, the Purple Lightning Mad Lion could still hold its own against him. Three days later, Su Yang was already pressing it back.

*What the hell.....*

"Master, take it easy! I'm at least your sidekick, aren't I? Do you really have to be this brutal!" The Purple Lightning Mad Lion looked at Su Yang, wreathed in fighting spirit, and remembering the terrifying punch from a moment ago, couldn't help but yelp in protest.

"Heh-heh, you'll be fine. I know your hide's tough, so this is the perfect chance to put that body art Senior Great Sage taught you into practice."

"Don't run, come again!" Su Yang's fists swung wide, his bare torso harder and more powerful than before.

Those swinging fists were more fearsome than three days ago: even the smaller meteorites could be shattered by a single punch from Su Yang. One could only imagine the sheer force packed into a blow like that.

The Purple Lightning Mad Lion, all its kingly aura long gone, watched Su Yang relish the brawl and could only dodge as best it could.

The thrill of pure power was something Su Yang couldn't get enough of.

Sweat poured off him, but it couldn't tamp down the hot blood in his veins. Fight, fight, fight! Only in combat could Su Yang forget everything, forget his hatred.

And at that very moment, the spiritual energy within Su Yang seemed to catch the same fever, surging in growth at an astonishing rate.

From beneath his golden body, spiritual energy raged outward.

It coalesced into a pillar of spiritual light that surged straight up into the starry sky.

*Hot damn! Don't tell me this kid's about to break through to the Tri-Profound Realm?* The Great Sage stared at Su Yang's current state, equally unable to believe it.

He had thought it would be impressive enough if Su Yang managed to push the power of the Battle Sage Art as far as possible within three days. It hadn't crossed his mind that Su Yang could break through to the Tri-Profound Realm right here and now, skipping past several minor stages within the realm in the process.....

Su Yang seemed to sense the changes in his own spiritual energy too. He froze on the spot, no longer striking.

Instead he felt the raging power of the spiritual energy within him, his expression flushed with excitement. "This is Tri-Profound qi! I'm breaking through!"

The Purple Lightning Mad Lion had long since frozen in place, staring at Su Yang slack-jawed. "Mon... monster....."

"Kid, get back to this Great Sage's side, now. The fight just now must have triggered some kind of energy reaction between the Supreme Blood inside you and the Battle Sage Art. That's what's letting you break through in just three days. A chance like this comes once in a thousand years. Make sure you seize it." The Great Sage's voice rang out across the starry sky.

At those words, Su Yang wasted no time. He turned into a streak of golden light and landed in front of the Great Sage's stone statue.

He dropped into a meditation posture, ready to meet the Tri-Profound Realm breakthrough head-on!

His boiling Supreme Blood and the surging rush of spiritual energy now formed two streams of power that burst out of him at once.

At the same time, within his dantian, the spiritual energy was slowly differentiating.

Three distinct streams of power took shape in an instant, fine as threads, flowing through Su Yang's eight extraordinary meridians and out into every limb and bone.

Soon those three threads merged with his spiritual energy and his Supreme Blood, fusing into one. A surge of overwhelming spiritual power erupted out of him.

Boom!

A vast aura billowed outward. The sheer extremity of the power twisted Su Yang's face into a mask of pain.

What had been a single ordinary pillar of spiritual energy now split into three pillars, each with a distinct aura: one of Tri-Profound Power, one of pure spiritual energy, and one of golden Supreme Blood.

The terrifying sight made even the Great Sage cry out. "Well I'll be! This kid's Tri-Profound Power has gone and fused with the strength of his Supreme Blood. He's got one more weapon in his arsenal than any other Tri-Profound cultivator out there. This kid is, hands down, the most off-the-charts brat this Great Sage has ever laid eyes on!"

"With power that terrifying, even a Divine Martial Realm cultivator probably couldn't do a thing to him."

Su Yang didn't know about the changes happening to him in that moment. All he could feel was the power inside him, seemingly inexhaustible, and his boiling Supreme Blood pushing him toward some kind of release.

"Aaaah!"

Boom!

With a single roar from Su Yang, an overwhelming aura burst out of him in an instant.

Breakthrough! Into the Tri-Profound Realm!

The Purple Lightning Mad Lion had moved past shock and into pure numbness.

It had no doubt left now. Its master Su Yang was no ordinary human.

Monstrous didn't begin to cover it.....

Su Yang rose, closed his eyes, and felt the surging power churn inside him. He drew a deep breath and slowly opened his eyes. His gaze came out like a hail of blades, sharp with killing intent. "Wang Family. Wang Yiyi. This young master is coming down the mountain."

"Hahaha, good lad. Not bad. You've climbed faster than this Great Sage imagined."

"At your current strength, you can leave the mountain."

"But don't get careless. That girl carries the Fire Phoenix Bloodline, and now with your Supreme Bloodline added in, the fusion of those two bloodlines will give her no small fighting power."

"Remember: if you can take your revenge, you don't need to hold back. But if you run into other trouble, don't push too hard. A gentleman's revenge can wait ten years.[^1]"

"Stay alive. Only then can you become the strongest there is."

The Great Sage seemed to have foreseen something, his tone unusually grave.

At those words, Su Yang dropped to one knee. "Senior Great Sage may rest assured. This disciple knows what to do."

"And I'll be sure to stay alive."

With that, Su Yang rose and prepared to leave the stone world.

"Little lion, let's go. It's about time I took you out for a stroll." Su Yang walked over to the Purple Lightning Mad Lion and patted its powerful body.

"Roaaar! Finally getting out of here. Hahaha! This king's going to find that stinking lizard and have his revenge, hehheh." The Purple Lightning Mad Lion was visibly thrilled. The "stinking lizard" it spoke of was none other than the other rank-six demonic beast that had been fighting it for territory: the Poison Butterfly Spirit Crystal Lizard!

---

[^1]: 君子报仇，十年不晚 (jūn zǐ bào chóu, shí nián bù wǎn) — literally "a gentleman taking revenge, ten years is not late." A well-worn Chinese saying: a person of principle is patient about settling scores, willing to wait years for the right moment rather than acting rashly. The Great Sage's choice of this proverb here is half-warning, half-blessing — Su Yang has the strength to act, but the timing must serve him, not the other way around.

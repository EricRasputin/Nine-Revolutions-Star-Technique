# Nine Revolutions Star Scripture (九转星辰诀) - English Translation

Welcome to the unofficial fan translation repository for **Nine Revolutions Star Scripture**! This project uses AI-assisted translation to provide high-quality English translations of the novel.

## About the Novel

-   **Original Title:** 九转星辰诀
-   **Author:** My Dog-Skin Plaster (我的狗皮膏药)
-   **Genre:** Action, Eastern Fantasy, Martial Arts, Reincarnation, Xuanhuan
-   **Chapters:** 3,793 (Completed)
-   **Original Source:** [[Qidian]](https://www.qidian.com/book/1033472618/) | [[Qimao]](https://www.qimao.com/shuku/202636/) | [[Shuqi]](https://www.shuqi.com/book/8509569.html)

### Synopsis

A great emperor is reborn as a powerless, scorned son-in-law — but he carries the awakened memories of nine lifetimes. Peerless geniuses and ancient prodigies? Mere stepping stones on his path. With the mysterious Nine Revolutions Star Scripture in hand, he devours the spiritual energy of heaven and earth, forging an unstoppable rise through the realms. Sword in hand, he crushes all who stand before him across ten thousand worlds.

## How to Read

There are two ways to read this translation:

### 1. Download Ebooks (Official Releases)

We compile completed volumes into EPUB format.

1.  Navigate to the [**Releases Page**](../../releases).
2.  Find the release you want to download.
3.  Under the **"Assets"** dropdown for that release, click on the `.epub` file to download it.

### 2. Read Online

You can read the individual chapters directly in this repository. All translated chapters are located in the `translations` folder.

-   [**Browse All Chapters**](./translations/)

## How to Contribute

We welcome all contributions! Here are the best ways to contribute:

### 1. Report Typos and Errors

If you find a typo, grammatical error, or a mistranslation, please let us know by [**opening an Issue**](../../issues).

When reporting an issue, please try to:

-   Use a clear title (e.g., "Typo in Chapter 12").
-   Include the chapter number.
-   Quote the incorrect sentence.
-   Provide the correction if you know it.

### 2. Suggest Terminology

We maintain a [**Glossary**](./assets/glossary.json) to keep key terms (names, places, cultivation ranks, techniques) consistent.
If you have a suggestion for a term, you can [open an Issue](../../issues) to discuss the term.
